Quadratic drag formula for a solid sphere at high Reynolds numbers.

\(\vec{F}_d = -C_d \frac{\pi}{8} d^2 \cdot \frac{1}{2} \rho_f |\vec{V}| \vec{V}\)

where \(\vec{V}\) is the relative velocity: \(\vec{V} = \vec{V}_p - \vec{V}_f\). The subscript p refers to a particle. The subscript f refers to the ambient fluid.

The specific value of \(C_d\) chosen here is the high Reynolds number limit of equation 8 of Loth.

References: E. Loth, “Quasi-steady shape and drag of deformable bubbles and drops,” International Journal of Multiphase Flow, vol. 34, no. 6, pp. 523–546, Jun. 2008, doi: 10.1016/j.ijmultiphaseflow.2007.08.010. (See eq. 4.)

Implements ablate::particles::drag::DragModel.
